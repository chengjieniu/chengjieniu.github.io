My name is Chengjie Niu (牛成洁). I am a Ph.D. candidate at National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), supervised by Yueshan Xiong (熊岳山) and Kai Xu (徐凯). From 2020, I am a remote visiting PhD student under the supervision of Hao (Richard) Zhang (张皓). I received my master degree in computer science from NUDT. I got my bachelor degree at Shandong University , advised by Chenglei Yang. My research interests include Computer Vision and Computer Graphics. [CV]
